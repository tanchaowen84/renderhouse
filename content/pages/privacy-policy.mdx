*Last updated: December 21, 2025*

## Overview

Render House is operated by an independent developer ("we", "us"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information we collect

### Account data
- Name and email from Google or GitHub OAuth (via Better Auth)

### User content
- Images you upload
- Render outputs we generate

### Prompts and chat
- We do **not** store chat messages or prompts.

### Payments
- Payments are handled by Creem (merchant of record). We receive only limited billing status and plan details needed to provide service access.

### Analytics
- We use Plausible, Google Analytics, and Microsoft Clarity. These may collect usage data through cookies.

### Access logs
- We do **not** store access logs (for example IP address, device fingerprint, or browser details).

## How we use information
- Provide and improve the rendering service
- Authenticate users and secure accounts
- Operate the product and maintain reliability
- Process subscriptions and manage access
- Generate renders by sending your inputs to our AI providers

## How we share information
We do **not** sell or rent your personal data.

We share data only with service providers required to deliver the service:
- Authentication: Google, GitHub, Better Auth
- AI inference: OpenRouter, fal.ai
- Storage: Cloudflare R2
- Payments: Creem
- Analytics: Plausible, Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity

## Data retention
- Uploaded images and generated renders are stored only to provide the service.
- When you delete a project, associated files are deleted immediately.
- Prompts and chat messages are not stored.

You can delete projects directly in the product or request deletion by emailing support@renderhouse.org.

## Security
We take reasonable measures to protect your data. No system is completely secure.

## Children
Render House is not intended for children under 13. If local law requires a higher minimum age, that higher age applies.

## Changes
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date above will be revised when changes are made.

## Contact
Questions? Email support@renderhouse.org.
