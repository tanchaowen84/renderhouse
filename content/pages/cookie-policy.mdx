## Overview

Render House ("we", "us") uses cookies and similar technologies to operate and improve the service. By using Render House, you agree to the use of cookies as described here.

## Cookies we use

We only use analytics cookies:

- Plausible Analytics
- Google Analytics
- Microsoft Clarity

These tools help us understand how users interact with Render House so we can improve usability and performance.

## No cookie banner

We do not display a cookie consent banner. By using the service, you consent to the analytics cookies listed above.

## Managing cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings and delete them at any time. Disabling cookies may reduce analytics collection but should not prevent core functionality.
